About Abdul Abdi

My name is Abdul Abdi. I am a parent, community advocate, and the current Minneapolis Public Schools Board Director representing District 1. I bring strong experience in public service, financial leadership, and community engagement, and I am committed to ensuring every student has the opportunity to succeed.

I currently serve as Chair of the Finance Committee, where I work to strengthen classrooms, support educators, and expand student programs. As a lifelong advocate and a parent, I understand firsthand the importance of safe, inclusive, and high-quality public schools.

I am running for re-election to continue improving student achievement, listening to families, supporting our educators, and delivering results for our students and neighborhoods.
